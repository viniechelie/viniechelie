About Us

Baby’s Bistro first opened on February 10, 2021. Back then, there was barely no milkshake shop in our town and my aunt’s girlfriend crave milkshake for almost every single day. That’s when she had thought of building her own milkshake shop for her girlfriend and at the same time make a living. As she was inspired by her girlfriend to have her own milkshake shop, she expresses the sweetness of her love by making milkshakes.
