Our SweeTea Story

Baby’s Bistro first opened on February 10, 2021. Back then, there was barely no milkshake shop in our town and my aunt’s girlfriend crave milkshake for almost every single day. It is founded by Miggy GC. Gonzales, Baby’s Bistro is a combination of the owner's mother’s name “Baby” and “Bistro” denotes a small, casual restaurant with an inexpensive, simple menu and the endearing tagline “Have a SweeTea!” symbolizing her beloved girlfriend, who serves as an inspiration of continuously making shakes with love.

Embark on a journey to our enchanting bistro, where every milkshake is a symphony of flavors crafted with love. Picture yourself enveloped in a warm embrace of nostalgia as you savor the creamy richness that dances on your palate. In every sip lies a story waiting to be told, a memory waiting to be made. Whether you're seeking a refreshing treat on a sunny day or a sweet escape with loved ones, our milkshake haven promises an unforgettable experience.
